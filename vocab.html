The Startup Dictionary

A list of terms/acronyms that are relevant including some specifically for college and Columbia companies.

Acronyms

IP Intellectual Property (Patents, Trademarks, etc..)
VC's Venture Capitalists
MVP Minimal Viable Product

Columbia/NYC
CVC Columbia Venture Competition
CEO Columbia Entrepreneurship Organization
DRF Dorm Room Fund, a student oriented firm
RDV Rough Draft Ventures, a student oriented firm

Funding Terms

Pre-seed Round Early funding often with no valuation in mind
Seed Round First round of funding, often with equity
Series A Major round of funding usually to fund expansion
Series B, C, D.. Subsequent Major Rounds of funding
Angel Investor A single person who invests a grand amount
Convertible Note Money given as debt that converts to equity at later valuation
